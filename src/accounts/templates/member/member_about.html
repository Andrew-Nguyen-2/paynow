About

PayNow

PayNow is an online payment system that allows organizations to easily pay their membership dues. It can be used by organizations to handle their monetary needs. It has two different user statuses: administrator and member. The administrator is able to send invoices to the members and budget the expected amount as well as the current amount. The website features visual aids to see how much of the total is going to different needs of the administratorâ€™s choosing and how much each member has paid of the total amount owed.
